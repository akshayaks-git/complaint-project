    Online Complaint Registration system

    It helps the public in knowing their place details and getting their problems solved in online without going to the officer regularly until the problem is solved.

    ABOUT US

    This system the public can save his time and eradicate corruption in govt offices.

    We want to develop an application for complaint management system where public can register complaints for street light, water pipe leakage, rain water drainage, road reconstruction and garbage system. To transform the existing manual complaint management system into an automate system. For the better management of complaints to improve efficiency. By this system the public can save his time and eradicate corruption in government officers. Its main purpose is to provide a smart and easy way through web application for Complaint registration and eradicating Bribing system and thus to prevent corruption.
